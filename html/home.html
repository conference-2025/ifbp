The 1st International Conference on Future Business Perspectives (IFBP 2025)

"Resilience, Digital Innovation, and Sustainability: Empowering Businesses for Global Growth"

15th-17th December 2025, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

Welcome to IFBP 2025

The 1st International Conference on Future Business Perspectives (IFBP 2025) invites scholars, industry leaders, policymakers, and innovators from across the globe to explore the transformative potential of "Resilience, Digital Innovation, and Sustainability: Empowering Businesses for Global Growth." In a world marked by rapid technological advancements, shifting market dynamics, and pressing global challenges, the conference serves as a vital platform to exchange ideas, showcase cutting-edge research, and foster collaborative solutions. Hosted at the prestigious Nguyen Tat Thanh University, Vietnam, IFBP 2025 aims to address the critical intersection of resilience, innovation, and sustainability, equipping businesses with the tools to navigate uncertainties, embrace technological opportunities, and drive sustainable growth on a global scale. Join us to shape the future of business in this era of unprecedented change and opportunity.

Conference Thematic Focus

The thematic focus of the conference on "Resilience, Digital Innovation, and Sustainability: Empowering Businesses for Global Growth" is pivotal for global businesses navigating today’s complex and interconnected landscape. Resilience equips organisations to withstand disruptions, whether from economic volatility, geopolitical tensions, or global crises like climate change and pandemics. Digital innovation drives efficiency, agility, and competitive advantage, enabling businesses to adapt to rapidly evolving markets and customer expectations. Sustainability, increasingly a core imperative, ensures that growth aligns with environmental and social responsibility, fostering trust and long-term value creation. Together, these themes provide a holistic framework for businesses to thrive, empowering them to seize global opportunities while addressing critical challenges of the modern era.

Mission and Vision

Mission Statement

The International Conference on Future Business Perspectives (ICFBP) aims to bring together global thought leaders, educators, innovators, and professionals to explore and discuss emerging trends, technologies, and strategies shaping the future of business. Our mission is to foster a collaborative environment for knowledge exchange, inspire innovative thinking, and drive sustainable business practices that address the challenges and opportunities of a rapidly evolving global market. By connecting diverse perspectives from academia, industry, and government, we aim to create actionable insights that will empower businesses to thrive in the future.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to be the leading global platform for advancing the future of business, where ideas, innovations, and solutions converge to shape the next generation of industries. We envision a world where businesses are adaptable to change and catalysts for positive social, environmental, and economic transformation. Through this conference, we aspire to inspire a collective movement toward a more sustainable, inclusive, and technologically driven global business ecosystem that benefits society at large.
