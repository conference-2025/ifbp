Conference Venue

Ho Chi Minh City (HCMC) is at the heart of Vietnam's economic and technological growth, pivotal in shaping the regional and global business landscape. As a thriving hub for finance, industry, and innovation, the city is the perfect place to host the 1st International Conference on Future Business Perspectives (IFBP). With its dynamic mix of business, technology, and entrepreneurship, HCMC offers a unique opportunity for global leaders to engage with Vietnam’s rapidly evolving market. The city's strategic location in Southeast Asia and its growing reputation as a tech powerhouse make it an ideal setting for discussing the future of business and technology. In addition, HCMC’s state-of-the-art infrastructure, vibrant startup ecosystem, and strong international connections create an environment ripe for collaboration and the exchange of groundbreaking ideas. Hosting the IFBP here will not only showcase Vietnam’s forward-thinking approach to business but also highlight HCMC’s role as a key driver of innovation in the region and beyond.

The city is a thriving centre for innovation, particularly in the high-tech sector, with a growing number of tech startups, research centres, and multinational corporations establishing their presence. HCMC's dynamic business environment, combined with its strategic location and robust infrastructure, positions it as a key player in Vietnam’s ongoing digital transformation and its ambitions to become a regional leader in technology and innovation. As the largest city and commercial hub of Vietnam, HCMC is central to the country's economic performance, contributing significantly to its GDP, industrial output, and export activities. The city’s strategic location along the Saigon River, combined with a modern port infrastructure, positions it as a vital logistics and manufacturing hub, facilitating trade between Vietnam and global markets.

Nguyen Tat Thanh University (NTTU), located in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, is an ideal venue for hosting the 1st International Conference on Future Business Perspectives (ICFBP). Renowned for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation, NTTU offers modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art facilities, and a vibrant academic environment. Its strategic location in Vietnam’s bustling economic hub provides convenient access to global transportation networks, making it an attractive destination for international participants. The university’s focus on fostering global collaboration, alongside its strengths in technology, sustainability, and business studies, aligns seamlessly with themes of international relevance. Additionally, Vietnam's dynamic economic growth and cultural richness offer a unique backdrop, ensuring a meaningful and engaging conference experience.
